Tourism Data: Visitor Arrivals to Peru

This page presents data showing the monthly number of international visitors arriving in Peru. The data highlights long-term trends, seasonal patterns, and the impact of global events on tourism.

Key Information

  • Peru recorded 284,236 international visitor arrivals in October 2025, compared to 292,917 in the previous month.
  • Visitor arrival data is reported monthly and covers the period from January 2002 to October 2025.
  • The highest number of arrivals was recorded in July 2019, reaching 412,415 visitors.
  • The lowest recorded value occurred in September 2020, when arrivals dropped to zero due to global travel restrictions.
  • Data is provided by the Ministry of Foreign Trade and Tourism and includes overseas Peruvian visitors.

Source: CEIC Data – Ministry of Foreign Trade and Tourism (Peru)
